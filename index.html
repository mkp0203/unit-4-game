Crystal Collector

Rules

1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2. There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal, you will be adding a specific amount of points to your "Total Score."

3. You win the game by matching your "Total Score" to the "Goal" number. You lose the game if your "Total Score" surpasses the "Goal" number.

4. The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked.

5. Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal is randomized.
